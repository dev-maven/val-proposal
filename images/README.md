# Photo Placeholders

This folder contains placeholders for the photos used throughout the Valentine's website.

## Required Photos:

### **photo1.jpg** - Profile Photo

- **Location:** Stage 2 (The Opening Letter)
- **Suggested:** A beautiful photo of your partner or a photo of both of you
- **Recommended size:** 400x400px (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### **photo2.jpg** - Memory 1

- **Location:** Stage 4 (Our Story - Memory Lane)
- **Suggested:** A photo representing "The moment I knew you were special"
- **Recommended size:** 600x600px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### **photo3.jpg** - Memory 2

- **Location:** Stage 4 (Our Story - Memory Lane)
- **Suggested:** A photo representing "When you made me laugh until my cheeks hurt"
- **Recommended size:** 600x600px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### **photo4.jpg** - Memory 3

- **Location:** Stage 4 (Our Story - Memory Lane)
- **Suggested:** A photo representing "Every adventure is better with you"
- **Recommended size:** 600x600px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

### **photo5.jpg** - Couple Photo

- **Location:** Stage 8 (Celebration)
- **Suggested:** Your favorite photo of you two together
- **Recommended size:** 1000x800px (landscape) or 800x1000px (portrait)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG

## How to Add Your Photos:

1. Choose your favorite photos
2. Rename them to match the required names above (photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, etc.)
3. Replace the placeholder files in this folder with your photos
4. Make sure the file names match exactly (case-sensitive)

## Tips:

- Use high-quality images for the best experience
- Square photos work best for photo1, photo2, photo3, and photo4
- The website is responsive, so photos will scale nicely on all devices
- If you want to use PNG files for transparency, just rename them with .jpg extension or update the HTML accordingly
